🌐 Networking / CompTIA Track

Start with CompTIA fundamentals then advance to Cisco CCNA → CCNP → CCIE. Networking is the foundation of all cloud, security, and DevOps work. Estimated path: 6–24 months depending on experience.

🐧 Linux / Red Hat Track

Linux is the foundation of all servers, containers, and cloud. Path: Linux basics → LFCS → RHCSA → RHCE → RHCA. Fully performance-based exams — no multiple choice. Estimated path: 6–18 months.
