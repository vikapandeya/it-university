Private Endpoints & Service Endpoints

VNet isolation for PaaS services
Private Endpoint
WhatNIC with private IP in your VNet
DNSPrivate DNS Zone required (auto or manual)
TrafficAll traffic stays inside VNet/backbone
ScopePer resource instance
Service Endpoint
WhatRoute to PaaS over Azure backbone
IPSource IP is VNet private IP
RestrictionAdd VNet to service firewall allow-list
ScopeWhole subnet to whole service
Private Endpoint > Service Endpoint for security. Private EP: traffic never leaves backbone, accessible cross-region. Service EP: subnet-level, same region.
