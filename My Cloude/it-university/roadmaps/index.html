Associate
Linux Foundation Certified SysAdmin
LFCS
💰 $395 📝 Performance-based ⏱️ 2 hrs ✅ 66%
Hands-on exam in a live Linux environment. User/group management, storage, networking, services, containers. No multiple choice — prove it.

  • practice with VMs (Ubuntu/CentOS)

Professional
Red Hat Certified System Administrator
RHCSA (EX200)
💰 $400 📝 Performance-based ⏱️ 3 hrs ✅ 210/300
Most respected Linux cert for enterprise. Pure hands-on: 15-20 tasks in a live RHEL VM. SELinux, systemd, containers (Podman), storage, NFS.
Study Resources:
  • Sander van Vugt RHCSA 9 (O'Reilly)
  • KodeKloud RHCSA Labs
  • Linux Foundation Training

Red Hat Certified Engineer
RHCE (EX294)

Builds on RHCSA. Ansible automation focus: playbooks, roles, collections, Ansible Vault, managing RHEL systems at scale.
Study Resources:
  • Sander van Vugt RHCE 9
